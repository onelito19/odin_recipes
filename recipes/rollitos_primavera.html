Rollitos Primavera

Description

Chinese-style spring rolls (or Chinese rolls), with very different sizes, shapes and fillings, are a tremendously popular snack in Asian countries and the rest of the world. Its name is due to the fact that the rolls, originally, were prepared at the end of winter and the new spring vegetables arrived at the markets.

Ingredients

  • 12 papers of flour for rolls
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cm of fresh ginger in pieces
  • 1 small onion
  • 50 of cabbage
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 fresh shiitake mushrooms
  • 50 g of soy noodles or vermicelli
  • 50 g of sprouts fresh soybeans
  • 15 ml of soy sauce
  • ground white pepper
  • 5 g of sugar
  • sunflower oil
  • 1 egg
